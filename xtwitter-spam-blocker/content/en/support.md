# Support and Contact

If you need help with X/Twitter Spam Blocker, you can contact support by email, join the Discord community, or open an issue on GitHub.

## Email

For privacy questions, support requests, or product feedback, email:

[vocabey@gmail.com](mailto:vocabey@gmail.com)

## Discord Community

For product discussions, quick questions, and community feedback, join the Discord:

[https://discord.gg/Xja6KRNrG](https://discord.gg/Xja6KRNrG)

## GitHub Issues

For bugs, broken behavior, compatibility problems, or feature requests, open an issue:

[https://github.com/vocabey/vocabey.github.io/issues](https://github.com/vocabey/vocabey.github.io/issues)

## What to Include

When reporting a problem, include:

- Extension version.
- Chrome version.
- The type of X/Twitter page where the issue happened, such as timeline, notifications, search, tweet detail, or profile.
- What happened.
- What you expected to happen.
- Optional screenshot, if it does not expose private information.

## Please Do Not Share Sensitive Data

Do not send passwords, private messages, authentication tokens, private account data, or screenshots that expose sensitive personal information.

If a report involves a specific blocked account, include only the information needed to understand the issue.
