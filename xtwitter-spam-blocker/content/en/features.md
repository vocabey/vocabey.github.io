# X/Twitter Spam Blocker Features

X/Twitter Spam Blocker helps clean noisy X/Twitter pages by removing matching adult-spam replies, off-platform lure content, unwanted promotional replies, and high-risk account signals from your local browsing view.

It is built for people whose replies, notifications, or timelines are repeatedly interrupted by spam accounts and suspicious profile links. The goal is simple: make X/Twitter cleaner while keeping important account actions under your control.

## Why It Matters

Spam usually shows up when you are trying to read a thread, check notifications, or follow a conversation. Manually opening every suspicious profile is slow, and the same patterns often come back again and again.

X/Twitter Spam Blocker reduces that work by cleaning matching spam while you browse and collecting risky accounts into a review flow before any block action happens.

## Core Features

### Clean Spam While You Browse

The extension removes matching spam from supported X/Twitter responses before the page renders them. This helps reduce adult-spam replies, off-platform lure content, unwanted promotional replies, and similar noise without changing how you normally browse.

### Review Risky Accounts Before Blocking

High-risk accounts are collected in a floating review panel. You can inspect the candidates, choose which accounts to block, and confirm the action yourself.

Accounts you do not select are not blocked by the extension.

### See What Was Cleaned

The dashboard and Activity Log show what happened on this device, including cleaned tweets, high-risk accounts, successful blocks, and failed block attempts.

This makes the cleanup visible instead of leaving you guessing whether the extension is working.

### Local-First by Design

Settings, protection statistics, activity history, and safety status are stored locally on your device in the current version. You can clear local statistics and activity history from the extension settings when needed.

## Best For

X/Twitter Spam Blocker is best for:

- People who receive adult-spam replies or noisy notifications.
- Creators and active X/Twitter users who attract repetitive spam accounts.
- Users who want to reduce off-platform lure content or unwanted promotional replies.

## Current Scope

The current version is focused on adult-spam cleanup and review-before-blocking for high-risk spam accounts. It is not a general-purpose moderation system, full scam detector, or guarantee that every spam account will be found.
